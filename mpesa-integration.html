M-Pesa Integrations

Seamless integrations of local payment gateways (Safaricom M-Pesa Daraja C2B/B2C, STK Push) for your business.

Automate Your Customer Payments

Manually tracking Till or Paybill receipts wastes time and causes order log errors. We deploy automated API callbacks directly connecting your Safaricom business credentials to your online store or POS database. Receive instant notifications the moment a transaction completes.

M-Pesa STK Push (Daraja API)

Customers enter their mobile number at checkout, triggering an automatic M-Pesa PIN prompt directly on their phone screen to secure and complete payment.

C2B & Paybill Reconciliations

Automatically log paybill payments to invoice rows on your database server. Perfect for school portals, rent managers, and bulk service utilities.

B2C Bulk Disbursals

Integrate pay-out systems to disburse funds to supplier accounts, employee salaries, or refund customer balances safely through software controls.
